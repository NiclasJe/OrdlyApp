About Ordly

Do you love word games? Then Ordly is the game for you! Every day you get a new word to figure out in six tries. Use your best strategies, let your brain work, and see how quickly you can crack today’s word!

How does it work?

Type in a word and get instant feedback!

  • Blue letter = Correct letter in the correct place.
  • Orange letter = Letter is in the word but in the wrong place.
  • Black letter = Letter is not in the word at all.

Save your progress

Create an account with your email and a username to save your statistics and see how you improve over time!

Daily challenges

A new word every day – how many can you solve in a row?
